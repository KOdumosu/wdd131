Efluxe Innovations – Website Project Plan

A professional creative and technology solutions Brand.

Site Name

Efluxe Innovations

I chose this name because it blends "E" (from personal and digital meaning) with “Fluxe,” representing flow, creativity, innovation, and digital transformation. It expresses a premium and modern identity suitable for creative, branding, photography, and Tech services.

Optional domain possibility: efluxeinnovations.com (availability not required for assignment)

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Efluxe Innovations website is to present a professional online presence for a creative and technology-driven brand. The site will showcase services such as graphics, Software & Tech Solutions, Photography, branding, digital Solutions, and community projects. It will also provide clients with information, project portfolios, and contact options.

Scenarios

  • How can I contact Efluxe Innovations to book branding or photography services?
  • Where can I view the portfolio of past creative or digital transformation projects?
  • What digital services does Efluxe Innovations provide for businesses looking to grow online?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Gold/Amber – #C7A008

Used for: Headings, accents, buttons, highlights, borders.

Secondary Color: Dark Gray – #1A1A1A

Used for: Header background, footer, dark sections.

Light Background: #F5F5F5

Used for: Main page background and readable content blocks.

Typography Color: White (#FFFFFF) and Dark Text (#222222)

These colors give the site a modern, premium, and creative visual identity suitable for a digital service brand.

Typography

Main Font (Headings): Montserrat

Used for all headings to give a clean, modern, tech-inspired look.

Body Font: Poppins

Used for paragraphs and body text for easy readability and professional appearance.

Both fonts are Google Fonts and work well for a creative and technology-focused company.
